HELP FIGHT AGAINST POVERTY

At the Roderic and Sophia Baker Foundation, we are committed to improving the lives of communities globally through dedicated support and sustainable initiatives. Our mission is to provide immediate aid and long-term solutions in several key areas. We are dedicated to eradicating global poverty by empowering families and communities to achieve self-sufficiency. In partnership with global allies, we deliver both immediate relief and sustainable development initiatives.

Approach goes beyond temporary aid. We focus on education and agricultural training, equipping communities to grow their own food and fostering skills for long-term prosperity. Our support persists until these communities can sustain themselves, ensuring lasting impact.

Our Programs

Child Protection & Education

Every child deserves a safe place to learn and grow. Our initiatives include building community schoolhouses that serve as secure learning environments for children. These spaces are more than just buildings; they are beacons of hope for the future of the communities we serve.

Countries Served: Uganda | Sierra Leone | Liberia | Jamaica

We will accept donations locally for non-perishable goods example (can goods, rice, dry beans, tooth paste, toilet paper, toothbrushes, deodorant, lotion, shampoo, conditioner, noodles, aspirin, tri-antibiotic ointment, pampers, and feminine products).

Simply call and we will arrange a pick-up!

We also accept checks, please make all checks out to the Roderic and Sophia Foundation. Call or email for EIN number.

We also accept donations via cash app $rasbf21 or follow the PayPal link in the menu.
