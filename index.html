Our Programs


Agriculture & Food Security

Food security is fundamental for community stability and growth. We teach families to farm sustainable crops, not only to feed themselves but also to create opportunities for income. Our programs are designed to foster self-reliance and promote agricultural education, which are critical in building a foundation for a healthier future.

Child Protection & Education

Every child deserves a safe place to learn and grow. Our initiatives include building community schoolhouses that serve as secure learning environments for children. These spaces are more than just buildings; they are beacons of hope for the future of the communities we serve.

HealthCare

Health is a critical component of a thriving community. We focus on bringing medical supplies to areas hardest hit by poverty, aiming to improve overall health outcomes and provide communities with better chances at a prosperous life.

We will accept donate locally for non-perishable goods example (can goods, rice, dry beans, tooth paste, toilet paper, toothbrushes, deodorant, lotion, shampoo, conditioner, noodles, aspirin, tri-antibiotic ointment, pampers, and feminine products).

Simply call and we will arrange a pick-up!

We also accept checks, please make all checks out to the Roderic and Sophia Foundation. Call or email for EIN number.

We also accept donations via cash app $rasbf21 or follow the PayPal link in the menu.

About Us

We are dedicated to eradicating global poverty by empowering families and communities to achieve self-sufficiency. In partnership with global allies, we deliver both immediate relief and sustainable development initiatives.

Our approach goes beyond temporary aid. We focus on education and agricultural training, equipping communities to grow their own food and fostering skills for long-term prosperity. Our support persists until these communities can sustain themselves, ensuring lasting impact.
